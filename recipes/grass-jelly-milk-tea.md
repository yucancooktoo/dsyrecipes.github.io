# Grass Jelly Milk Tea

## info  
* 5 minutes 
* 2 servings  

## ingredients
* grass jelly in can
* Oolong tea or any tea bag you like
* milk
* honey or sugar

## steps  
1. 1 can grass jelly
2. ½ cup brewed oolong tea cooled
3. 1 ½ very cold milk
4. honey or sugar to taste
